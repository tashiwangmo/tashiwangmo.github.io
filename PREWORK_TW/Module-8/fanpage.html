Biography

Beyoncé Giselle Knowles-Carter was Born and raised in Houston, Texas. She is married to rapper Jay Z and has 3 kids namely Blue Ivy, Rumi and Sir Carter.

Beyoncé rose to fame in the late 1990s as lead singer of the R&B girl-group Destiny's Child. She released her debut album, Dangerously in Love in 2003. The album established her as a solo artist worldwide, earned five Grammy Awards, and featured the Billboard Hot 100 number one singles "Crazy in Love" and "Baby Boy".

Throughout her career, Beyoncé has sold 100 million records worldwide, making her one of the world's best-selling music artists.[3][4] She has won 22 Grammy Awards and is the most nominated woman in the award's history. She is also the most awarded artist at the MTV Video Music Awards, with 24 wins.

Her discography as a solo artist:

  • Dangerously in Love (2003)
  • B'Day (2006)
  • I Am... Sasha Fierce (2008)
  • 4 (2011)
  • Beyoncé (2013)
  • Lemonade (2016)
